Electrical Repair Services

Fast, accurate diagnosis and repair of any electrical issue — residential or commercial.

Fix It Right the First Time

Electrical problems don't just inconvenience you — they can be dangerous. Flickering lights, tripping breakers, warm outlets, and dead circuits are all signs that something needs immediate attention. Tikal Electric provides fast, thorough electrical repair services to keep you safe.

Our licensed electricians arrive fully equipped to diagnose and fix most issues on the spot. We explain the problem clearly, present your options, and get the repair done right — guaranteed.

Common Repairs We Handle:

  • Dead outlets and switches
  • Flickering or dimming lights
  • Tripping circuit breakers
  • Faulty wiring diagnosis and repair
  • Burning smells or sparking outlets
  • GFCI outlet troubleshooting
  • Surge protection repair
