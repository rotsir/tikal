Our Story: Building on a Foundation of Strength

Named after the ancient Mayan city of Tikal — a symbol of incredible engineering, endurance, and architectural mastery — Tikal Electric was founded with a simple mission: to bring that same level of precision and reliability to every electrical project we touch.

What started as a small, family-owned operation has grown into one of the most trusted electrical service providers in the region. Our team of licensed electricians brings decades of combined experience to every job, from simple outlet repairs to complex commercial installations.

We believe that great electrical work isn't just about wires and circuits — it's about safety, trust, and building lasting relationships with our community. When you hire Tikal Electric, you're hiring a team that treats your home or business like their own.
