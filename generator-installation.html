Generator Installation Services

Never lose power again — professional generator setup for homes and businesses.

Power Through Any Outage

Power outages are more than just an inconvenience — they can disrupt your life, damage your electronics, and even pose safety risks. A professionally installed generator ensures you always have the power you need, no matter what nature throws your way.

Tikal Electric provides end-to-end generator services — from helping you choose the right unit for your needs, to professional installation, transfer switch wiring, and ongoing maintenance plans.

What's Included:

  • Whole-home standby generator installation
  • Portable generator hookup and wiring
  • Automatic transfer switch installation
  • Commercial generator systems
  • Generator sizing and load analysis
  • Permit acquisition and utility coordination
  • Annual maintenance and service plans
